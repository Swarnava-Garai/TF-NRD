# TF-NRD: Transcription Factors Non-Redundant Dataset

TF-NRD is a curated **non-redundant dataset of transcription factor (TF) sequences and structures**, designed to support structural and functional analysis of TF assemblies across domains of life.

---

## 🔬 Overview

Transcription factors (TFs) are key regulators of gene expression. They interact with various biomolecules, including DNA, RNA, DNA–RNA hybrids, and proteins, to perform their regulatory functions.

The **TF-NRD dataset** provides a comprehensive and non-redundant collection of TFs to facilitate:

- Structural analysis of TF complexes  
- Understanding TF binding mechanisms  
- Development of predictive computational models  
- Exploration of TF interaction diversity  

---

## ✨ Highlights

- TFs play critical roles in gene regulation  
- TFs interact with:
  - DNA  
  - RNA  
  - DNA–RNA hybrids  
  - Protein partners  
- Structural diversity of TFs enables unique interaction interfaces  
- Provides an atlas for studying TF assemblies across biological domains  
- Useful for **computational prediction and modeling of TF interactions**
